About Me

I am a fourth-year Bachelor's student in Computer Engineering at the University of Mohaghegh Ardabili, advised by Prof. Shahram Jamali and Prof. Hamid Bahador. I completed my high school Diploma degree from Allameh Helli High School. My research interests lie in the field of Computer Vision and Machine Learning, particularly working with Generative Models and Vision Transformers. test test
